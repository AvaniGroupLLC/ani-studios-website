About

"Photography is the story you fail to put into words."

The Studio

Ani Studios is a luxury hospitality photography studio based in Dallas, Texas. Founded by Creative Director Heather Nicole, the studio specializes in creating elevated visual narratives for hotels, resorts, restaurants, and lifestyle brands across the globe. From the Maldives to Manhattan, our work captures the quiet details and grand moments that define world-class hospitality.

Creative Director

Heather Nicole brings an editorial eye and a deep understanding of the hospitality industry to every project. Her approach is rooted in natural light, authentic atmosphere, and an obsessive attention to the details that make each property unique. She has photographed properties across four continents, working with independent boutique hotels and global luxury brands alike.

Our Approach

We believe the best hospitality photography doesn't just show a space — it makes you feel what it's like to be there. Our process begins with understanding the soul of each property: its architecture, its light, its guests, its story. We then translate that understanding into images that elevate brand perception and inspire travel.
