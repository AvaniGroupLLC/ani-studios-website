Our Story

Where architecture meets artistry

ANI Studios was founded by Heather Nicole and Avetik Babayan with a singular vision: to capture the soul of the world's most extraordinary spaces.

We believe every building tells a story — our job is to make sure it's unforgettable. From five-star resorts in the Maldives to architectural landmarks in Toronto, we bring a cinematic eye to every frame.

Our approach is grounded in patience, intentionality, and a deep respect for the spaces we photograph. We don't simply document properties — we reveal their character.

"We don't chase trends. We chase light."
— ANI Studios
Our Approach

Considered. Cinematic. Enduring.

Every project begins with understanding — the architecture, the light, the intent behind the design. We spend time with each space before we ever raise a camera, studying how it breathes at different hours, how materials respond to natural light, and how people move through its rooms.

The result is photography that feels inevitable rather than staged. Images that hotels, resorts, and architectural firms use for years, not seasons. Visual stories that honor the vision of the architects and designers who created these spaces.

We work with a small, curated roster of clients who share our commitment to excellence. If you value photography as an investment rather than an expense, we'd love to hear from you.
